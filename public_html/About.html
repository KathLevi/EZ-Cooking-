About 

 This new cookbook application can help you organize your recipes in one convenient location. 
You can download recipes from the internet into your application or you can just write them yourself.
Another fun part of this application is that you can pick your recipes for the week and then create
a grocery list from those recipes so you'll know in advance all of the ingredients that you will need. 
You can even search for a recipe based on available ingredients in your pantry or fridge. How convenient!
